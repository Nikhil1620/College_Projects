# HR Analytics Dashboard

This repository presents a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard visualizing key HR metrics and workforce insights. The dashboard empowers HR teams and organizational leaders to monitor employee attrition, satisfaction, demographic distribution, and education trends for informed decision-making.

## Features

- **Core HR Metrics**: Tracks total employee count (1,470), attrition count (237), attrition rate (16.12%), active employees (1,233), and average employee age (37).[1]
- **Attrition Breakdown**:
  - Departmental attrition proportions (HR, R&D, Sales) for targeted interventions.
  - Attrition by gender highlighting distinctions between male (150) and female (87) exits.
  - Attrition rates further segmented by age group for detailed analysis.[1]
- **Workforce Demographics**:
  - Detailed age distribution of employees, visualized as a bar chart.
  - Average age highlighted for age-based workforce planning.[1]
- **Job Satisfaction**:
  - Cross-tab of job role vs. satisfaction rating, giving a granular view of engagement by department and function.[1]
- **Education Insights**:
  - Bar chart analysis of attrition by educational field, spotlighting vulnerable talent pipelines (e.g., Life Sciences, Medical).[1]

## Dashboard Insights

- Sales and R&D departments face the highest attrition rates, especially among younger, early-career employees.
- Male employees show higher attrition compared to females, particularly in the 25-34 age range.[1]
- Job satisfaction levels, as captured by departmental ratings, highlight key satisfaction gaps and retention priorities.[1]
- Life Sciences and Medical educational backgrounds are the most affected by attrition, necessitating special focus for retention planning.[1]
